Stay Healthy

Exercise

Eat Healthy

Formula

MET value multiplied by weight in kilograms tells you calories burned per hour (MET*weight in kg = calories per hour)

Foods

Green Tea, Berries, Eggs, Salmon, Ginger, Grapefruit, Celery, Broccoli, Apple

Exercises

Walking, Jogging, Cycling, Yoga, Swimming, Pilates, Weight Training

Routine

Early Morning Jog, At least 20 sit-ups per day, and any type of Weight Training each day.
Nutritional Facts
  • 1 gram of fat has 9 calories (kcal)
  • 1 gram of alcohol has 7 calories (kcal)
  • 1 gram of carbohydrate (sugars and starches) has 4 calories (kcal)
  • While fiber is a carbohydrate, it is not easily digested by the body, so -calories from fiber are estimated by the U.S. Food and Drug -Administration to be 1.5 calories for 1 gram.
